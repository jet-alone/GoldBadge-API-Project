"Mix-It-Up" Recipe Search

A helpful resource for chefs with artist's block.


    Why Mix-It-Up?


    Cooking and baking is an art. Chefs know this, bakers know this, and like any form or medium of art, there is always a risk of a "block" - a moment in time, or perhaps a mental state where one finds oneself unable to come up with new ideas for their art. Painters, musicians, and of course, chefs all know this from experience. This "Mix-It-Up" web page is here to help! To the left-hand side, chefs struggling to conjure new ideas and recipes can input a search for different kinds of ingredients and foods, returning ten articles containing interesting recipes meant to get the gears in your brain turning and help you leap to your next great culinary idea. Simply type in an ingredient you'd like to work with (e.g., basil, oregano, blueberries, thyme) or even a dish (e.g, spaghetti, meatloaf, cake, pie) to retrieve ten recipes associated with that ingredient or dish. You can even try different culinary terms (e.g., fillet, deglaze, baste, bisque) to return recipes that fall into that category. Try it out!
